Example 3 : Enjoy the simplicity of entities and collections
Find information about Mister Dupond
MrDupond = ds.Employee.find('lastName = ' + 'Dupond');
Who are the collegues of Mr Dupond ?
MrDupond.company.employees
Who is the manager level 2 of MrDupond ?
MrDupond.manager.manager
How many collegues of him live in his country ?
MrDupond.company.employees.query('country.ID = ' + MrDupond.country.ID).count();
